# AEO Audit: threadstomillions.com
**Date:** April 15, 2026

## Executive Summary

The site is almost entirely unoptimized for Answer Engine Optimization. Google AI Overviews, featured snippets, and People Also Ask boxes require specific content structures that are nearly absent across the blog.

Five most critical gaps:
1. Zero FAQ schema on any blog post (only on homepage)
2. No HowTo schema on any instructional post (14 posts qualify)
3. Most posts bury the answer after 3-4 paragraphs of context
4. Headers are topic labels, not questions
5. No Speakable schema anywhere
